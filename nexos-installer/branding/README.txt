NexOS Branding
==============

grub-background.png
  Place your PNG image here (recommended: 1920x1080 or 1024x768).
  This will be used as the GRUB background for:
    - The NexOS installer ISO
    - The installed NexOS system

  Supported formats: PNG, JPEG (rename to .png)
  GRUB supports: 640x480, 800x600, 1024x768, 1280x720, 1920x1080
